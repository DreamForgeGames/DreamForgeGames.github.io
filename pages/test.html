Our Story

We are a group of students who occasionally come up with crazy ideas and publish them.
We needed a brand name for this publication. We're not a real company, but we found a good one: DreamForge Games
The whole thing happened in 2024, but we've never actually implemented it. We did come up with a few ideas, like StoryCraft, but abandoned them due to some problems.
Over time, many ideas have accumulated, and so we're starting another attempt in 2025.
